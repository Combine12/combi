SCP-3996
rating: -16+–x

Item #: SCP-3996

Object Class: Keter

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-3996 is locked in a 25x25x25 metre room made out of solid iron and its space time holders should never be turned off even when testing on the SCP. Anyone who does not ask for O5 permission shall be terminated.

In case of a breach SCP-3996 should have 24/7 surveillance and there should always be guards near the entrance of the Containment Chamber. When the power fails be sure to evacuate the building and only enter when 3996 is back in its chamber and poses no threat by Epsilon 11.

Description: SCP 3996 is black hole which takes in any object or person and swallows them, never to be seen again leaving no trace behind of its/there existence. If the space time holders are turned off the entity will start to move with no traceable pattern and swallowing everything in its path, the more things it swallows the bigger it gets and the bigger it gets the more its out of control.

Addendum: If the SCP gets out of control contact on of the hierarchy to launch the alpha omega warhead and all MTF units need to arrive on the scene immediately with the supervision of the Alpha 1 division.
