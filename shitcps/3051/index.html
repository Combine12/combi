Scp 3051

item #:Scp-3051
Object class: Euclid

Special containment procedures:
Scp-3051 is to be contained in a ten meter by ten meter by ten meter containment chamber, comprised of led, sheet, metal, iron and copper for Scp-3051 is unfamiliar with these materials, and becomes greatly confused when around them. The inside of the containment chamber is to be like that of a maze. Mazes appear to confuse scp-3051 and reduce the risk of a containment breach.

Description:
Scp-3051 appears to be a Human skeleton approximately three meters tall and one hundred and twenty centimetres wide. Scp-3051 is comprised of a mixture of bonemeal and normal human bones.

Scp-3051 was discovered in North America in [data expunged] scp-3051 was found by security team [\classified\] and was brought to the attention of the foundation after audio transmission cut out from security team [\classified/] after screaming was herad moments before audio transmission cut out. With one of the members screaming out the words "it's a walking skeleton a fu***ng walking skeleton. Oh sh*t it's attacking general [data expunged].

Scp-3051 grows about sixteen centimetres of flesh on it every week. If the flesh begins to cover any limbs scp-3051 is to moved to a three meter by three meter by three meter containment chamber, once scp-3051 is inside, the containment chamber is to the be filled with any form of acid that can do significant damage to human flesh. Once all the flesh is removed from scp-3051 scp-3051 is to be electrocuted (to temporarily stun it) and moved back into its main containment chamber. For more information about why the flesh of scp-3051 is to be removed see incident: [data expunged].

If scp-3051 breaks containment the area is to be evacuated immediately to ensure that no personnel are injured or killed by scp-3051.
